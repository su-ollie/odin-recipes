Broccolli Cheddar Soup

Description:

Comfort in a bowl is much easier than you think! This creamy soup is perfect for a cold winter night or any other occasion. It could be made as an appetizer or as the main course of any meal!

The broccoli and freshly grated cheddar make an unstoppable duo that will have you asking for seconds!
